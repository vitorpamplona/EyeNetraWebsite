We see you! Now come change the world!

We have come together around a singular challenge: to revolutionize access to vision care on this planet. We’ve worked hard to create a workspace and company culture that values diversity, promotes experimentation and empathy, and emboldens our people to do great things.

Our pace is fast and agile - we thrive in a highly dynamic work environment where independence, risk, and generative thinking are embraced and encouraged. At any given moment, you’ll find us testing our own eyesight and developing new prototypes on Chewbacca, our beloved 3D printer. We work together, play together, and regularly devour ethnic food together. Plus, you can’t beat the location. We’re near the largest maker communities in the USA, and walking distance to MIT and Harvard.

We look for talent that exemplifies passion, ingenuity, curiosity, courage and selflessness. Help us unleash our human potential and make history - together, we will change the world.
