The Mobile Refraction Kit

This all-inclusive kit takes your practice on the road to conduct refractions and collect the basic measurements required for glasses anywhere. It combines each device in the EyeNetra Product Suite for an unique testing experience with cutting-edge mobile technology.

This set includes:

  • 1 Netra (Auto-Refractor)
  • 1 Netrometer (Auto-lensometer/Focimeter)
  • 1 Netropter (Phoropter/Trial Lens kit)
  • 1 Calibrated Smartphone (EyeNetra applications pre-installed)
  • 1 Charger, Extended Battery and Cable for the Smartphone
  • 1 Travel Case for all products.
