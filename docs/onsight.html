OnSightSM

Vision Care Delivered

Vision care that your employees will love.

By bringing OnSight into your workplace, employers offer their staff convenience, time savings, improved productivity and employee satisfaction - and a really cool experience. OnSight comes to you with licensed optometrists and opticians, FDA-registered smartphone-powered optometry devices, and HIPAA-compliant cloud-based software.

We’ve got a solution that works for you.

We’ve got a solution that works for you.

OnSight brings optometrists and opticians to your workplace, sets up an exam room almost anywhere (open space, break room, conference room), schedules employees ahead of time, performs comprehensive eye exams, writes prescriptions, offers eyeglasses and contact lenses for purchase, and handles reimbursements.

Comprehensive Eye Exams are a Must!

We have partnered with licensed independent optometrists nationwide who perform in-person comprehensive eye examinations on every single patient. Our partner ODs use products of their choosing including EyeNetra products for refraction and lensometry as well as third party products to collect necessary measurements and examine eye health. Financial incentives are not tied to filing prescriptions.

Let us know if you would like to join the OD team or if you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions about our service. If you already have our devices, you are more than welcome to participate. We’d love to hear from you!
