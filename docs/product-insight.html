Insight

THE MOBILE CLINIC MANAGER

Manage event scheduling, reminders, intake forms, eye exams, prescriptions and payments in one place.

With an intuitive interface and unlimited cloud-based storage, Insight handles everything from prescription writing and data analytics to online booking for your patients. Designed for mobile events, Insight is an efficient way to manage all you need to prescribe outside your main clinic.


5 Step Workflow for Mobile Clinics:

  • CREATE AN EVENT

    Patients book appointments on the event page

  • CAPTURE INTAKE & CREDIT CARD

    Patients fill health questionnaire and pay on-line

  • CAPTURE RX MEASUREMENTS

    At the event, go through the 21-point exam

  • PRESCRIBE & E-MAIL

    Fill out the form and send to the patient

  • CHECKOUT & ARCHIVE

    Charge the final amount and send receipts
